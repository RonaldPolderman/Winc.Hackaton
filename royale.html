Gym Royale

The gym i currently train most often

The gym i currently train the most days of the week. Usually around 4 to 6 times a week depending how my knee holds up or the time i have.
There are 3 trainers during a normal week depending on what day of the week and if they have fighter obligations.
Paul Lamoth, Alex Chadud da Silva, Vahid Shahbazi each of them with their own specialty. Where Paul is a combination of techniques and stamina Alex leans more to the stamina side of things without losing sight of techniques. Vahid is the youngest of the three. His muay thai background and him living, fighting and traing for years in Thailand shows in his approach. He leans heavily towards techniques and the exact execution of them.
