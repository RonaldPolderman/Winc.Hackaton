Superpro Koh Samui Thailand

My vacation adventure

After approximately three years of Pancration training i decided it was time to follow a childhood dream and go to Thailand for almost four weeks to go and train Muay Thai.
Superpro Samui had connections with Pancration so the choise was made. I went early 2020 to get myself killed with the old disciplines in the ways of Muay Thai. After a 20 hour flight the humidity hit me like a brick wall. I loved it.
The next day my first heavybag training at Superpro commenced at 7 am. After walking down the stairs before the training even started i was sweating like a mule that tries to keep up with an arabian full blood horse.
After warming up with some running, jumping and skip roping the lessons began. They where divided into some thai padwork, heavybag and some one on one techniques.

There was, compared to the gyms i trained at thus far in Dutchieland, a great variaty of Kru. Usually there where 8 to 10 different kru present for each training session. A couple of the ones i remember most are:


  1. Charoon
  2. Keng
  3. Beau
  4. Noi
  5. Chain
  6. and the Kru i did most padwork with is Nueng
