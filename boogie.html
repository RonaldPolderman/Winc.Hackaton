Boogieland

The gym i no longer train

During the pandemic Boogieland had the great idea to put heavy bags around the city so people could keep training outside. Obviously a great alternative since all gym's where closed and it was prohibited to train in group form.
Because of this great brainchild of Boogieland, the closing of Pancration and the end of the 1st lockdown at that time i gave Boogieland a chance and started training there.
The two people i encountered the most for trainings where Maartje Piersma and Emma Schouwenaar. Neither of them seem to have a wikipedia page so for any fans of either of them, there's your chance
Both of them are great ladies with a solid knowledge about kickboxing altho, probably because of the mostly students without any experiences in martial arts the classes consist mostly of stamina exersices and basic combinations.
Nothing wrong with stamina and basic combinations but overall it wasn't my cup of tea.
The second lockdown hit us and everything closed again. When the second lockdown ended Pancration reopend her doors so back to Pancration it was for me.
